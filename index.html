Inquiry Brief

Research question: How can AI assist in game design?

Why it matters: Many new coders struggle to get started with game design and often feel overwhelmed. I am curious about the tension between AI helping beginners learn faster and the risk of them relying on it too much. AI could make coding more accessible, but it might also limit real understanding.

What I need to learn: I need to understand how AI tools currently help with coding in game design, what tasks they are best at, and where they fall short. I also want to learn how beginners actually use AI and whether it improves learning or creates dependency. Finally, I need to explore how to balance AI assistance with real skill development.

Possible final outcomes: A tool that helps new coders build simple game mechanics with AI guidance; a learning system that teaches coding step-by-step while using AI as support; or a project about the risks of over-reliance on AI and how to balance learning with assistance.
